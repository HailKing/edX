This was the first book I read at the age of 12/13 years old. At that time I had already known the character of Sherlock from common sense, as a synonym for a clever person but never really had contact with. So when I read I couldn't stop until then end when I found out the killer was right behind my nose. The way crime is pictured is wonderful, if we may say such thing about such horror, and the tools mr.Holmes uses to discover everything is something really out the curve when goes to intelligence. So this is a book I would strongly recommend everyone to read.
