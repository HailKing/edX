This is for certain one of the best TV shows ever made. Furthermore the psychology of the characters is very well worked in this serie and that is the biggest golden key of it, because we can see how they sufer because of the troubles they go through and how they get affected by them

The shows is well written and makes the readers feel happy as the serie readapts the written novels and shows us how maybe the story could have been written if the author lived in our time.
